A Little Something About Me!

Hello! My name is Tsarina Patnaik, and I'm a data enthusiast with a splash of color! As a master's student in Data Analytics at Northeastern University, Boston, I juggle numbers by day and add vibrant creativity by night. My approach to tech is as unique as a palette of paint, blending the logical rigor of engineering with the warmth of management studies and a hint of dance rhythm. From Python to the bustling streets of Boston and the traditions of India, I carry a world of perspectives in my toolkit. I'm all about merging analysis with empathy, crunching numbers, connecting hearts, and celebrating the quirks that make us unique. Actively seeking jobs and internships, I invite you to join me on this journey as we make data come alive together!
